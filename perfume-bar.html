The Experience

A Scent Story in Four Steps

Our fragrance specialists guide guests through every step. No expertise needed, just curiosity.

1

Smell the Notes

Guests explore a curated palette of 12 to 20 fragrance notes, from fresh citrus to warm woody bases.

2

Build Your Formula

Guided by our specialist, they select 3–5 notes and blend them into their own unique signature scent.

3

Bottle It Up

The blend is carefully poured into their chosen bottle (vial, 30ml or 50ml spray) and sealed.

4

Label It

Guests name their fragrance and receive a custom-printed label. Their very own perfume, in minutes.
