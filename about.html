About Us

We make sure every guest leaves
your event with something they made.

Event Studio started with one belief: people remember what they made, not what they were handed. A branded tote bag gets forgotten. A tote bag they watched get printed with their name in front of them? That is a story they tell.

We build two rental activation boxes for events in the Netherlands: The Clicker Box and The Hot Press Booth. Order online, set up in minutes, return after. No staff needed at the station.

What We Stand For

Built on Three Principles

01

Experiences Over Objects

Anyone can give away a branded item. We create a moment, something guests participate in and feel invested in. The object is just the proof it happened.

02

Craft at Scale

Our activations are fast enough for hundreds of guests, yet detailed enough to feel like a one-of-a-kind moment for each one. No shortcuts, no compromises.

03

Seamless for You

We design, pack and ship everything. You open the box at your venue and you are ready in 20 minutes. The return label is already inside for after your event.

The Team

A small Dutch team
that takes events seriously.

We are a small team based in the Netherlands, building activation boxes and running event installations for brands and event planners. We know what guests love, what planners stress about, and how to make both sides happy.

Every box we ship is designed and packed by us personally. If you choose Full Service, the specialist at your event has run this activation dozens of times.
