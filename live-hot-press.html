The Experience

45 Seconds to
a Custom Keepsake

One of the most visually compelling activations. Guests love watching the press come down.

1

Choose Your Item

Guests select from canvas totes, pouches, cosmetic bags, laptop sleeves and more in multiple sizes.

2

Pick Your Design

Choose from preset designs, your event's branding, or a custom name, applied via heat transfer vinyl.

3

Watch the Press

The heat press comes down at high temperature. In 45 seconds, a crisp, durable print is fused to the canvas. Permanent and washable.

What We Offer

Three Ways to Personalize

Name & Initials

Classic and clean. Guests' first name, initials or a short phrase in a bold font of their choice.

Brand Design

Your company logo, event artwork or a custom design transferred in full color, great for corporate gifting.

Event Theme Print

We create a series of preset designs themed around your event. Guests pick their favorite to take home.

Quotes & Words

A meaningful quote, a motivational word, or a fun phrase, printed large and bold on canvas.
