FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

What is included in the activation price?
Everything. Your quote covers the full artisan team, all materials and consumables, equipment, transport, setup, live event support and packdown. You focus on hosting. We handle the rest.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend 4 to 6 weeks ahead for peak seasons (April to June, September to November). That said, we regularly accommodate last-minute requests. Just reach out and we will do our best.
Can I run multiple activations at one event?
Absolutely. Many clients run 2 to 3 activations simultaneously for different audience segments. We coordinate all setups and can build a bundled package with combined pricing.
Do you travel to our venue?
Yes. We travel to your venue with all equipment. For events outside our standard coverage area, a travel surcharge may apply. This will be clearly noted in your quote upfront.
How long does setup take?
Most activations are event-ready in 30 to 60 minutes. We arrive 90 minutes before your event to ensure everything is perfect before your first guests arrive.
Is there a minimum guest count?
No strict minimum. Activations work best with 30 or more guests, but we do intimate private events too. Contact us and we will put together a package that makes sense for your group size.
