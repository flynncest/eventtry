FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

What is included in the activation price?
Everything in the box. Your order includes all keys or stickers, the equipment, branded table cover, setup guide and a prepaid PostNL return label. For Full Service bookings, the on-site specialist, setup and breakdown are also included.
How far in advance should I book?
Order at least 7 days before your event so we have time to design and pack your box. For peak seasons (April to June, September to November) we recommend 3 to 4 weeks. Last-minute? Call us directly and we will see what we can do.
Can I run multiple activations at one event?
Yes. Order both boxes and run two stations side by side. Guests can do both. We offer a discount on combined orders. Ask about it in the form.
Do you travel to our venue?
For Activation Box orders, we ship directly to your venue via PostNL. For Full Service bookings, our team travels to your location. A travel surcharge may apply outside our standard area and will be noted clearly in your quote.
How long does setup take?
The Activation Box sets up in about 20 minutes. Lay out the table cover, arrange the items using the included guide, and you are ready. For Full Service bookings, our team arrives 60 to 90 minutes early.
Is there a minimum guest count?
No minimum. The Starter Box works well from 20 guests. The Popular Box is sized for up to 75. Tell us your guest count in the quote form and we will recommend the right size.
