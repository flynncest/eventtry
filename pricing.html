How It Works

Two ways to bring the activation to your event.

Whether you want everything handled for you or prefer to run it yourself, we have a format that fits. Both deliver the same live, personalized guest experience.

A new way to activate

Your activation, shipped.
Your event, your setup.

The Activation Box is a fully equipped live personalization experience delivered straight to your door via PostNL. No crew, no scheduling, no logistics headaches. Everything your guests need is packed, branded, and ready to go.

Open the box. Follow the guide. Run a live activation that feels professional, because everything in it is. When your event is done, ship it back in the same box using the return label inside.

  • All materials for every guest, pre-counted and pre-sorted
  • Your custom design applied to every piece before shipping
  • Branded table cover and station layout sheet
  • Step-by-step activation guide with illustrated setup
  • Return shipping label included, no extra cost
